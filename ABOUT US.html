ABOUT US

At Cambridge Institute of Technology - North Campus, we help you choose a career path that aligns with your interests, empowers you with the necessary skills and provides a head start in your career with a globally recognised degree.

The college is located off the International Airport Road in Bangalore. Despite being well-connected, the campus offers a serene atmosphere away from the city's hustle and bustle, devoid of distractions, making it an ideal environment for learning. This tranquil setting is further enhanced by world-class infrastructure and a team of highly qualified, experienced faculty members. The colleges’ vision is to become a hub for engineering excellence, innovation and societal impact, nurturing engineers to solve complex challenges through research, quality education and a culture of collaboration and integrity. The institute's progressive teaching approach encourages innovation and skill development, equipping students to excel in a dynamic and competitive global landscape. The highly industrious placements department ensure excellent placements. Cambridge Institute of technology North campus students have the opportunity to interview with more than 120 companies with 100% placements for all eligible students. Leading industries and corporations have consistently shown a strong preference for our students. This is evident in the multiple job offers received by every third student on campus. Employers recognize Cambridge North graduates as smart, driven, and capable—qualities that give them a significant edge in the professional world. Over the years, the college has become a top choice for aspiring engineering students from across Karnataka, as well as from other parts of India and neighbouring countries like Nepal.

The college has spacious labs and classrooms, furnished with state-of-the-art infrastructure to effectively deliver the coursework. Additionally, the college hosts a variety of national and international conferences, seminars, tech talks, subject matter expert interactions, and hands-on workshops for its students. Prominent educationists, celebrities, technocrats and industry experts regularly visit the campus, sharing their wealth of experience and insights with the students.
Many of our faculties are alumni of prestigious institutions such as IITs, IISc, and internationally renowned universities. With an average teaching experience of 15 years, our faculty includes textbook authors, award-winning educators and academic toppers, all committed to excellence in teaching and mentoring. Our students shine bright on various platforms, securing funding, winning competitions and earning accolades nationally. Their success serves as a testament to their talent and dedication and the quality of education imparted at our institution. Special support is also provided to slower learners to ensure inclusive growth and academic success.
If you are looking for a college to hone and enrich your skills and also achieve great heights of success, Cambridge Institute of Technology North Campus is the place to be.
